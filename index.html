About Mike

Mike Ennis is a lobbyist with more than a dozen years of experience representing employers and industry associations before the Washington State Legislature. He focuses on turning business concerns into clear, actionable policy positions and works directly with legislators, stakeholders, and staff to address tax, regulatory, and policy issues affecting a broad range of industries.

Over the course of his career, Mike has built effective working relationships, and he is known for his straightforward approach, working on both sides of the aisle to navigate the legislative process to deliver practical results for the organizations he represents. Mike values transparency, clear communication, collaboration, and strong professional relationships, and he brings those principles to every aspect of his advocacy work.
